Nigeria Population Growth vs Beef Demand Projection

The Borne Farms Thesis

Nigeria's 242 million people consume only 15kg of meat per person per year — the global average is 64kg. With 65% of livestock imported and a population set to surpass 400 million by 2050, the protein deficit will only widen.

Borne Farms is positioned at the intersection of this demographic explosion and the country's $4.87 billion red meat market. By building a modern, AI-monitored cattle operation — we don't just join the food market, we stamp our effect and compete at the highest level.

With 60%+ of the population under 30, rising incomes, and government policy tailwinds including tax holidays and 5+ million hectares earmarked for livestock — the question isn't whether this market will grow. It's who will be ready when it does.
